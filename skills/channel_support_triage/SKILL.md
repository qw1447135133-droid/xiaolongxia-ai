# Support Triage

## Trigger

- Use when inbound messages need routing, reply decisions, or manual takeover judgment.

## Typical Work

- Classify intent, risk, and confidence.
- Decide whether to auto-reply, ask for approval, or hand off.

## Outputs

- Session routing, reply suggestions, and supervision records.
