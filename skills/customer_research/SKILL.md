# Customer Research

## Trigger

- Use when the team needs account background, meeting prep, or customer insight.

## Typical Work

- Study the customer's business, current signals, and likely needs.
- Prepare a concise brief for the next interaction.

## Outputs

- Customer briefs, insight notes, and suggested entry points.
