# Ticket Resolution

## Trigger

- Use when a ticket needs diagnosis, next-step planning, or a closure path.

## Typical Work

- Summarize the issue, rank urgency, and propose handling steps.
- Draft customer-facing updates and closure notes.

## Outputs

- Ticket summaries, handling plans, and closeout messaging.
