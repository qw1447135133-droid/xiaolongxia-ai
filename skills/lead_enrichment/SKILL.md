# Lead Enrichment

## Trigger

- Use when a lead needs more background, missing fields, or better outreach context.

## Typical Work

- Gather public company and customer clues.
- Fill missing context before the next follow-up step.

## Outputs

- Lead profiles, completed fields, and outreach rationale.
