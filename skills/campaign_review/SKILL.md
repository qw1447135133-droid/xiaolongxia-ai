# Campaign Review

## Trigger

- Use when a campaign or content release needs a retrospective.

## Typical Work

- Summarize what happened, what worked, and what failed.
- Turn outcomes into improvement actions for the next cycle.

## Outputs

- Review summaries, issue lists, and improvement actions.
