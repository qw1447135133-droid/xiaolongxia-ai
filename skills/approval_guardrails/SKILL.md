# Approval Guardrails

## Trigger

- Use when a task includes risky outbound actions, privileged operations, or human approval gates.

## Typical Work

- Judge whether the action can continue automatically.
- Stop low-confidence or high-risk steps at a manual boundary.

## Outputs

- Approval decisions, risk levels, and stop conditions.
